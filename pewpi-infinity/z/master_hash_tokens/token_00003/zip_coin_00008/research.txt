the linear discriminant analysis (lda) algorithm is used. several experiments were conducted on the large multispectral database (blue, green, red, and infrared) of the university of hong kong. as result, distinguished and high results were obtained where it was proved that, the blue spectrum is superior to all spectra perfectly. subjects: image and video processing (eess.iv) cite as: arxiv:2401.00008 [eess.iv] (or arxiv:2401.00008v1 [eess.iv] for this version) https://doi.org/10.48550/arxiv.2401.00008 focus to learn more arxiv-issued doi via datacite journal reference: 2022 ieee 2nd international maghreb meeting of the conference on sciences and techniques of automatic control and computer engineering (mi-sta), pp. 506 - 510 related doi : https://doi.org/10.1109/mi-sta54861.2022.9837520 focus to learn more doi(s) linking to related resources submission history from: alhaam aalariyibi ms.
